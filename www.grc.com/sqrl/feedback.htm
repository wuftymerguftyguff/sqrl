A highly secure, comprehensive, easy-to-use replacement
for usernames, passwords, reminders, one-time-code
authenticators . . . and everything else.
SQRL Feedback

Please use the web form below to send us any feedback — thoughts, ideas, questions, suggestions, or whatnot — but please, only regarding these SQRL pages.

PLEASE NOTE: I will be DELIGHTED to receive any and all comments and questions about SQRL. However, time constraints may limit my engagement in extensive one-to-one conversations. For big topic “forum” issues, a one-to-one dialog is inefficient.

GRC maintains an extremely high-quality newsgroup facility for exactly this purpose, and there is very little doubt that active and worthwhile dialog will be continuously underway about SQRL over in the grc.sqrl newsgroup. You can view a read-only snapshot of the most recent newsgroup postings with that link. If you wish to participate, please see GRC's Discussions pages for information about setting up an NNTP newsreader for newsgroup access and participation.

Thanks for your understanding.

PLEASE DO NOT use this page for any other type of feedback.
We have other pages available to route other non-SQRL feedback,
questions, etc. to the proper person.  See the bottom of our
Home page for product sales and support contacts, and the
Security Now feedback page for Security Now feedback. Thanks!
